Hopper's Fables: Rubric (3 point evaluation)

(Oct 12 - Oct 17)

During this week our mentor introduced to us a 3 point rubric evaluation. And we will be updating our rubric during this week according to this 3 point evaluation format. This 3 point rubric evaluation is very helpful, I can see now clearlier the purpose of the rubric and the importance of it. This rubric system is specific and simple. I love it. Each member of the group did a 3-point system according to the part they were assinged and we have combined them to make a our pre-final rubric. The final rubric is our pre-final rubric with changes from our mentor.
